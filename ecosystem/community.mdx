## How to engage

- **Run the app** — the most direct contribution. Each contributor strengthens the DePIN.
- **Help newcomers** — Discord and Telegram are full of first-time users with hardware questions; experienced contributors are the best resource.
- **Send signal** — bug reports and feature requests in the support channels are read and prioritised. The roadmap reflects them.
- **Hold and stake GHX** — token holders receive GHXP multipliers and have access to community programs.
- **Build something** — [deAPI](https://deapi.ai) is open to anyone. If you build a bot, agent, or app, share it.
