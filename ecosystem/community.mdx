## How to engage

- **Run the AI App** — the most direct contribution. Every active GPU strengthens the DePIN and earns GUSD + GHXP.
- **Help newcomers** — Discord and Telegram are full of first-time users with hardware and setup questions; experienced contributors are the best resource.
- **Send signal** — bug reports and feature requests in the support channels are read and prioritized. The [roadmap](/roadmap) reflects them.
- **Hold and stake GHX** — holders receive GHXP multipliers in the AI App, plus up to **25% APR** at [staking.gamercoin.com](https://staking.gamercoin.com).
- **Compete in GHXP seasons** — active contributors share a GHX reward pool every season. Dates and eligibility are announced on [Discord](https://gamerhash.com/en/l/Myw09) and [Medium](https://medium.com/we-are-the-gamerhash). See [GHXP](/ai/ghxp#seasons).
- **Build on the network** — see [AI builders](/use-cases/ai-builders) for how developers and AI teams integrate GamerHash compute into their products.
