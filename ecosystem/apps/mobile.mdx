This ensures that you stay on top of your cryptocurrency mining activities and can monitor your profits in real-time. With this app, you'll have full control over your account settings, and you can make changes within the app itself without having to log into the website.

What's more, the app comes equipped with a built-in store that allows users to exchange their earned funds for an array of exciting rewards such as the latest games, prepaid cards, and other exciting perks. For those who prefer to hold onto their funds, it's easy to send your BTC and GHX from your GamerHash wallet to your private wallet.
