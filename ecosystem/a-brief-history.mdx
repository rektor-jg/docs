## Foundations

2 friends, 1 groundbreaking, innovative idea and a vision so clear it made all their doubts irrelevant - **that’s where it all started**.

To tell you the story of Gamerhash, we have to go way back to the year 2017 when the company was born. Bitcoin reaching a new all-time high price, NFTs just starting to gain mainstream momentum - the whole situation was drastically different. That’s when the two friends, now successful co-founders, brought the product of their imagination that GamerHash is to life. With a mission to provide people with a simple and free tool to reap the rewards of blockchain and knowing the technology they have up their sleeves will change the world of cryptomining forever, they began their journey in the ruthless world of web3 business and never looked back.

Fast forward to the year 2019 - at that point, the project was in full motion. Lots of work followed by lots of successes are things that best describe that time, especially as GamerHash was getting increasingly more popular. Achieving further and further milestones was only accelerating more ideas on what ways the whole project can be developed in the future, which led to a totally new creation - the **Play &Earn module**. Introduced as a possibility to increase earnings for users with low spec devices, became a platform loved by everyone. Starting from a few tasks, it kept on evolving over the years, to finally become what it is today: a wide variety of thousands of tasks allowing users to earn additional funds!

## Introducing GamerCoin (GHX)

However, 2019 brought even more changes - positive ones, of course, as it was also the year we introduced our crypto baby, the native fuel of our evergrowing ecosystem: **GamerCoin (GHX)**. 

Starting as a ERC-20 token only, it was released to the public in 2020 during the Token Generation Event (TGE). Quickly gaining popularity, on April 6th 2021 the token was already listed on Bithumb - world’s third largest (at that time) exchange. The token kept on growing alongside our ecosystem and that’s why, since May of 2021, we’ve enabled our users to make payments with it in our GH Store. Then, since April 2022, we’ve also introduced it as a main reward for completing Play&Earn tasks - and we only have more and more ideas that will allow us to further develop its utility! Now, as 2023 has already rolled around, our GamerCoin is available both on Kucoin exchange as well as on DEX exchanges (Pancakeswap and Uniswap). What’s even more, in the middle of 2022 the token was bridged to Binance Smart Chain and is available as a BEP-20 token on top of ERC-20.

## GamerHash Studio

In order to shed a bit of light on how our most recent history looks like, in 2022 we’ve partnered up with one of the biggest metaverse projects right now - **The Sandbox**. And while we always do awesome things with our partners, the outcome of this collaboration was truly something we didn’t see coming. Namely: we started creating our very first, own gaming experience! The name’s “TRAVERSE: Mars is Mine” and to tell the truth its release is coming very soon - and we’re working extremely hard to make it look like everything “us gamers” dreamed of!

Needless to say, the first heroes of the GamerHash story - the 2 friends - definitely have a lot to be proud of. After all these years, admiring everything they’ve built, it’s safe to say that if only they could, they’d do it all again in a heartbeat. However, of course, the road to victory wasn’t always easy. Challenges, problems, obstacles: they’re all a part of the deal when you’re blazing the trails for something that has never been done before. Nevertheless, with a tough spirit and a team of supporting people that deeply believes in the project’s success, you can achieve some great things - and that is exactly what happened with GamerHash. 

And although the taste of triumph has been very sweet, we never let it get to our heads. That’s because, as we all know, there’s no bigger mistake than resting on one’s laurels - and for GamerHash, it’s only the beginning of something even greater. For now, we just keep on building and knowing what we provide is a true innovation that can potentially benefit thousands of people, we always follow our motto: 

> _We are here for the gamers, We are the game changer, We are the GamerHash._

## GamerHash AI

We've been gamers all our lives. Over the past few years, we've also learned a lot about our users and the hardware that resides in their homes every day.

Additionally, for over 1.5 years, we've been fascinated by the **development of AI**. It turned out that we actually have all the tools to combine all these parts and create a revolutionary product that will solve many problems.

In **2023** , we started working on our application, which today is called **GamerHash AI**. Initially developed by our passionate developers, after just a few months, it was evident that there was tremendous potential here. With each passing month, the GamerHash AI application became our pride and joy.

After consulting with our partners, it turned out that our idea was spot on and in 2024, we released the **first version** of our **application**. Initially, it attracted active users and rewarded them with **GamerHash Experience Points (GHXP),** which currently are regularly exchanged for our **GamerCoin (GHX)** token. In **March 2024** , we released another update to our application, which included another module: **AI Image Generator**.

All we can say is that we're just getting started and more modules will be released very soon!
