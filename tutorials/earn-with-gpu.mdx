This builds on [Install the AI App](/tutorials/install-ai-app). It assumes you already have the app installed, signed in, and your GPU benchmarked. Time: **10 minutes** to set up; then it runs in the background.

## What this gets you

- **GUSD per workload** — credited automatically as inference jobs complete.
- **GHXP** — engagement points that multiply future earnings via the GHXP Boost.
- **Optional GHX boost** — hold GHX in MetaMask to accelerate GHXP accrual.

See [Earning model](/ai/earning) for the full mechanics.

## 1. Open the earning module

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sidebar → Earning">
    The earning section is separate from the local-AI modules. The dashboard explains what's on offer before you opt in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Read the disclosure">
    The module dispatches third-party workloads to your machine. Make sure you're comfortable with that before enabling — see [Privacy](/ai/privacy).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable the module">
    Toggle the **Earning** switch on. Your GPU joins the dispatch pool.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## 2. Set boundaries

<Steps>
  <Step title="Active hours">
    Pick hours of the day when the module dispatches. Common patterns:

    - **Always when idle** — earn whenever the GPU isn't busy.
    - **Off-hours only** — overnight + workday hours when you're not gaming.
    - **Off-peak electricity** — match your tariff's cheap window.
  </Step>
  <Step title="GPU usage cap">
    Default cap leaves headroom for other applications. Lower it if you notice frame drops in games while the module runs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Auto-pause on game launch (optional)">
    If your launcher is supported, the AI App can pause earning when a game starts and resume when it exits.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## 3. Maximise yield

<Steps>
  <Step title="Hold GHX for the boost">
    The single biggest yield multiplier. Hold any GHX in MetaMask under the same account — see [GHXP](/ai/ghxp). Boost applies automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run continuously">
    The longer the app runs without restart, the larger your hour-credited GHXP accrual. Restarting mid-hour drops that hour's pending points.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Don't run multiple machines under different accounts">
    GHXP and ranks consolidate per account. Run all your GPUs under one account — you'll rank up faster.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## 4. Track earnings

<Steps>
  <Step title="In-app dashboard">
    Live per-hour, per-day, per-month numbers. Workload count and average pay per workload.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Mobile app">
    The [Mobile app](/apps/mobile) shows the same numbers — useful when you're not at the PC.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Email digests">
    Weekly summaries land in the inbox you registered with. Toggle off if you don't want them.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## 5. Pull GUSD out

Three exit routes — pick at any time:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Convert to GHX">
    1. In-app **Wallet** → **Convert** → GUSD → GHX.
    2. Pick the chain (Ethereum / BNB Chain / Solana).
    3. Confirm. GHX lands in the wallet you've connected.
    4. From there, **HODL** or **stake** for up to 25% APR via [staking.gamercoin.com](https://staking.gamercoin.com).
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Withdraw USDC to Binance">
    1. Connect your Binance account if you haven't (one-time, in account settings).
    2. **Wallet** → **Withdraw USDC** → choose amount.
    3. Internal transfer to your Binance address. No GamerHash fee.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Spend in the Store">
    1. Browse the [Store](https://gamerhash.com/en/shop).
    2. At checkout, pick **GUSD** as the payment method.
    3. Enter your account PIN. Digital goods are delivered immediately.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Module is on, but I'm getting no workloads">
    Several causes:

    - **GPU class** — older / smaller GPUs only qualify for a subset of workloads. Run benchmarks; check the live workload pool size.
    - **Network demand** — check the latest [Community Update on Medium](https://medium.com/we-are-the-gamerhash) for demand signals.
    - **Connection issues** — restart the app; verify you're not behind a corporate VPN that blocks dispatch traffic.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I think my GHXP rank dropped">
    GHXP is non-decreasing for activity, but rank is *relative*. New high-engagement contributors can shift rank ordering. Check the leaderboard; consistency wins over time.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Game frame rate drops while the module is on">
    Lower the GPU usage cap or set the module to **Auto-pause on game launch**. Some games are extremely sensitive to background VRAM use.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="GUSD balance shows but conversion is unavailable">
    Conversion is occasionally throttled during platform maintenance. The Wallet page shows scheduled windows; usually resolves within an hour.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="USDC withdrawal stuck">
    Internal transfers to Binance settle within minutes. If stuck >24h, contact Discord `#support` with the transaction reference shown in the Wallet log.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
