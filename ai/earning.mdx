The earning module turns the AI App from a free local-AI workstation into a side income. You opt in once; the network does the rest.

## The mechanics

1. **You opt in** — toggle the earning module on. Set hours / GPU usage cap if you want.
2. **The network dispatches a workload** — text, image, video, embedding, transcription. Whatever fits your VRAM and is in the queue.
3. **Your GPU runs it** — typically seconds to a few minutes per workload, depending on type.
4. **You're paid in GUSD** — credited to your in-app wallet on completion. 1 GUSD ≈ 1 USD.
5. **GHXP rank multiplies the reward** — same workload pays more for higher-ranked contributors.

## What determines per-machine yield

Yield depends on a handful of variables — not all under your control:

- **GPU class & VRAM** — a 4080 unlocks higher-paying workload classes than a 3060.
- **Uptime** — more hours online = more workloads matched.
- **GHXP rank** — long-running, GHX-holding accounts earn more per workload.
- **Network demand** — pay scales with deAPI volume. As demand grows, contributor pay grows.
- **Region / latency** — some workloads have geographic preferences for low-latency dispatch.

The dashboard inside the AI App shows live per-hour earnings and historical totals.

## What this is *not*

- **Not mining.** No PoW, no hash rates, no algorithm tuning. The platform serves AI inference jobs.
- **Not crypto trading.** GUSD is a stable USD-equivalent credit; the GHX conversion is optional.
- **Not guaranteed.** Yield depends on demand. There are no fixed APRs on the earning side — APR exists only on the [staking](https://docs.gamercoin.com/tokenomics/staking) side.

## Newcomer head start

For the first **30 days** after registration, new contributors keep **100% of credited earnings**. After 30 days the standard rank-based contribution applies — see the in-app dashboard for current rates.

## Optimisation tips

- **Run the app continuously** — most pay comes from being online when a workload arrives.
- **Hold some GHX** for the GHXP Boost (see [GHXP](/ai/ghxp)).
- **Don't compete with gaming sessions** — set a GPU usage cap so the app doesn't fight your game for VRAM.
- **Update the driver and the app** — performance improvements ship constantly; out-of-date stacks miss workloads.
