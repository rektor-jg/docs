The AI App is GPU-bound. CPU and RAM matter less; what determines whether a model runs is **VRAM** and the **GPU generation**.

## Minimum

- **OS** — <Win /> 10 (22H2+) or 11
- **GPU** — NVIDIA RTX series with **8 GB VRAM** (e.g., RTX 3060, 4060, 5060)
- **Driver** — recent NVIDIA studio or game-ready driver. The app warns if too old.
- **Disk** — ~30 GB free for downloaded models (more if you keep many in the Model Center)
- **Network** — broadband; model downloads are large but one-off

8 GB unlocks: AI Chat (7B/8B quantized models), Stable Diffusion 1.5 / Sana Sprint, basic earning workloads.

## Recommended

- **GPU** — RTX 4070 / 5070 or better, **12–16 GB VRAM**
- 12 GB unlocks: 14B chat models, FLUX image generation, LTX-Video at 480p.
- 16 GB+ unlocks: 20B chat models (GPT-OSS-20B), high-res FLUX, longer LTX-Video clips, multimodal vision.

## High-end

- **GPU** — RTX 4090 / 5090 / Blackwell pro cards, **24 GB+ VRAM**
- Full Model Center access, highest-paying earning workloads, video at higher resolution / duration.

## What's not supported

- AMD GPUs in the AI App today (some experimental builds exist; default install is NVIDIA-only).
- macOS (the AI App is Windows-only; the [Mobile app](/apps/mobile) is on iOS/Android but for account management, not local AI).
- Linux — same as macOS for the AI App. Server-side roles via deAPI are different — see [deAPI](/deapi/overview).
- iGPUs — integrated graphics don't have enough VRAM.

## Fit by module

| Module | Min VRAM | Recommended |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Text-to-Text (chat, 7B/8B quant) | 8 GB | 12 GB+ |
| Text-to-Text (14B–20B) | 12 GB | 16 GB+ |
| Text-to-Image (SD, Sana Sprint) | 8 GB | 12 GB |
| Text-to-Image (FLUX, HiDream) | 12 GB | 16 GB+ |
| Image / Text → Video (LTX-Video) | 12 GB | 16 GB+ |
| Multimodal (vision LLMs) | 12 GB | 16 GB+ |
| Earning module | 8 GB | 12 GB+ for higher-pay workloads |

The Model Center inside the app shows live fit estimates per model on your hardware.

## Upgrading mid-stream

You can switch GPUs without re-creating your account. The AI App detects the new card on next start and re-runs the benchmark. GHXP and account state carry over.
