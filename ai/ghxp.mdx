**GHXP (GamerHash Experience Points)** is an in-app reputation system. It is **not a token** — there's no contract, no liquidity, no transferability. It's a multiplier on the rewards you earn from sharing GPU power, plus a key to community programs.

## How GHXP accrues

- **Every minute** the AI App is running, you accrue GHXP.
- Points appear as **Pending** during the active hour and are **credited to your account** at the top of each hour. Closing the app before the hour completes drops the pending points for that hour.
- Background runs count — the app does not need to be in the foreground.
- Surveys and one-off in-app activities (e.g., trying a new module, completing onboarding) award additional GHXP.

## The GHX Boost

GHX held in **MetaMask** under the same account that runs the AI App **multiplies** GHXP accrual. The more GHX you hold, the higher the multiplier — capped, but meaningful.

- Boost is checked periodically and applied automatically.
- You don't lock or stake the GHX for the boost — it just has to be in the wallet you've connected.
- Boost stacks with the engagement-time bonus.

## Why it exists

GHXP isn't convertible to GHX. It's a **multiplier on your share of the reward pool**. Two contributors with identical GPUs can earn very different amounts if one has run the app for months and one started yesterday — that's by design. It rewards consistency over speculation.

GHXP also unlocks:

- **Higher reward rank** when revenue is distributed to contributors.
- **Eligibility** for airdrops, contests, and community programs.
- **Visibility** in leaderboards (opt-in, by display name).

## What GHXP is *not*

- Not a token. No contract address. No market price.
- Not transferable. Tied to your GamerHash account.
- Not directly convertible. You don't trade GHXP for GHX — you use it to earn *more* GHX per workload.

## How to maximise it

1. **Run the app continuously when idle.** A pause loses pending GHXP for that hour.
2. **Hold GHX in MetaMask** for the boost.
3. **Use new modules** as they launch — onboarding tasks usually award one-off GHXP.
4. **Complete in-app surveys** when offered (typically 2,000 GHXP).

## Watch out for

- Closing the app mid-hour discards that hour's pending points. Schedule restarts at the hour boundary.
- The boost reads on-chain GHX in **MetaMask only** for now. Other wallet integrations are tracked on the roadmap.
- GHXP is per-account, not per-machine. Running multiple machines on the same account is fine — the points accumulate together.
