**GHXP (GamerHash Experience Points)** is an in-app reputation and reward-share system. It is **not a token** — no contract address, no liquidity, no transferability. Think of it as the engagement score that scales how much you take home from each workload, plus your ticket to seasonal GHX rewards and community programs.

## How GHXP accrues

- **Every minute** the AI App is running, you accrue GHXP.
- Points appear as **Pending** during the active hour and are **credited to your account** at the top of each hour. Closing the app before the hour completes drops the pending points for that hour.
- Background runs count — the app does not need to be in the foreground.

## The GHX Boost

GHX held in **MetaMask** or in your **in-app GHX wallet** (under the same GamerHash account that runs the AI App) **multiplies** GHXP accrual. The more GHX you hold across either, the higher the multiplier — capped, so whales don't dominate.

- Boost is checked periodically and applied automatically.
- You don't lock or stake the GHX for the boost — it just has to sit in the connected MetaMask or your in-app wallet.
- Holdings across both wallets count together toward the multiplier.
- Boost stacks with the engagement-time bonus.

## Why it exists

GHXP isn't convertible to GHX. It's a **multiplier on your share of the reward pool**. Two contributors with identical GPUs can earn very different amounts if one has run the app for months and one started yesterday — that's by design. It rewards consistency over speculation.

GHXP also unlocks:

- **Higher account level** in the AI App — scales your share when revenue is distributed to contributors.
- **Eligibility** for airdrops, contests, and community programs.

## Seasons

GHX rewards are distributed in **seasons that run every two months**. Each season the network sets aside a GHX pool, and contributors who lock their GHXP split it proportionally.

How it works:

1. **Lock your GHXP** — commit the GHXP you've accumulated to the current season. The amount you lock is your stake in that season's pool.
2. **Your share is proportional** — your slice = your locked GHXP ÷ total GHXP locked across all participants.
3. **Payout closes the season** — when the season ends, GHX is distributed automatically based on your share.

> **Example.** You lock 10,000 GHXP in a season where the total across all participants is 1,000,000 GHXP — that's a 1% share, and you receive 1% of the season's GHX pool when it closes.

Specific dates, pool sizes, and eligibility rules for each season are announced through the [community channels](https://gamerhash.com/en/l/Myw09) and on [Medium](https://medium.com/we-are-the-gamerhash).

## What GHXP is *not*

- Not a token. No contract address. No market price.
- Not transferable. Tied to your GamerHash account.
- Not directly convertible. You don't trade GHXP for GHX — you use it to earn *more* GHX per workload.

## How to maximize GHXP

1. **Keep the app running when the machine is idle.** A pause mid-hour drops pending GHXP for that hour.
2. **Hold GHX** — in MetaMask, in your in-app GHX wallet, or both. Holdings across both count toward the multiplier (up to the cap).
3. **Stack across machines** — GHXP is per-account, not per-machine, so multiple rigs on the same login pool their points together.
4. **Keep MetaMask connected** — if MetaMask stops being detected, the GHXP Boost turns off entirely until you reconnect.

## Watch out for

- **Closing the app mid-hour discards that hour's pending points.** Schedule restarts at the hour boundary, not in the middle.
- **MetaMask connection drops are silent and they kill the boost.** If MetaMask gets disconnected mid-session, you lose the GHXP Boost — reconnect promptly to restore it.
