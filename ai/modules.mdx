The AI App is modular. You don't have to use all of it — open the dashboard and pick what you want. Every module runs locally on your GPU.

## Text-to-Text (AI Chat)

Private chat with the latest open-source language models. Pick a model from the **Model Center** and chat with no usage caps and no data sent to a third-party server.

**Models available** — Llama 3 family, Qwen 3, Phi-4 (Microsoft), DeepSeek (R1, Coder), Gemma, Bielik, GPT-OSS-20B (OpenAI's open release).

**VRAM** — most quantized 7B/8B models run on 8 GB. 14B–20B models recommend 16 GB+. The app shows fit estimates per model.

**Use cases** — drafting, summarising, code generation, document Q&A, brainstorming. No telemetry on prompts or outputs.

## Text-to-Image

Generate images from prompts. Results stay locally on your PC; the app keeps a history per device.

**Models available** — Stable Diffusion (multiple checkpoints), FLUX (Schnell + LoRA style packs), Sana 1.6B, Sana Sprint, HiDream, DreamShaper, Astro GHX Style LoRA.

**VRAM** — 8 GB for SD 1.5 and Sana Sprint, 12 GB+ for FLUX and HiDream at higher resolutions.

**Controls** — image count per prompt, quality / steps, fidelity, negative prompts, full-screen preview.

**AI Image Booster** — optional paid feature (100 GHX). Improves prompt quality through a small auxiliary model.

## Image / Text → Video

Turn a still image or a text prompt into a short video clip, locally.

**Models available** — LTX-Video (latest LTX-2.x), with regular updates as new open-source video models ship.

**VRAM** — 12 GB minimum. 16 GB+ recommended for longer or higher-resolution clips.

**Controls** — duration, motion bias, resolution, "video booster" prompt enhancement.

## Voice — coming Q4 2026

**Text-to-Speech** and **Voice Transcription** modules are on the roadmap for Q4 2026. They will run locally with the same privacy model as everything else.

## Multimodal

Upload images or PDFs and chat with them. Useful for document Q&A, visual analysis, and multi-page extraction. Built on multimodal LLMs supported by the Model Center.

**VRAM** — 12 GB+ recommended for vision-capable models.

## Earning module

Opt in and your GPU joins the dispatch pool when idle. AI inference workloads from [deAPI](https://deapi.ai) consumers are routed to your machine; you're paid in **GUSD** per completed job.

You can:
- **Set hours** — only earn while you sleep, only on weekends, or always when idle.
- **Set GPU usage cap** — leave headroom for gaming.
- **Pause anytime** — no minimum commitment.

See [Earning model](/ai/earning) for what determines per-machine yield.

## Model Center

The hub inside the app for browsing, downloading, and switching models. Indicates VRAM fit for your hardware. New models land here regularly — recently: GPT-OSS-20B, Llama 3.x updates, FLUX LoRA style packs.
