GamerHash is a digital platform that provides gamers with an innovative way to earn rewards through the utilization of their computer processing power. In addition to mining, the platform offers the Play&Earn feature, which enables gamers to earn virtual currency by playing games.

## GamerHash AI

At GamerHash, gaming computers transcend their conventional use and become integral components of a groundbreaking ecosystem. This transformation is achieved by harnessing the immense computational power of gaming GPUs, traditionally used for high-resolution gaming experiences, and repurposing them for AI-driven tasks and blockchain operations. Users of the GamerHash platform contribute their idle gaming computer's processing power towards complex computations needed for AI research and development, including machine learning, data analysis, and other intensive tasks. In return for their contributions, users are rewarded with**GamerCoin (GHX)** , thereby incentivizing participation in a unique symbiosis between gaming and the evolving fields of artificial intelligence and decentralized computing

The Store's selection of products features popular brands such as Steam, Xbox, PlayStation, and more. This ensures that gamers have access to the latest and most popular games, as well as hardware and accessories that are essential to their gaming setup.

One of the unique features of the GamerHash Store is its ability to offer vouchers for offline stores, such as Żabka and Biedronka, in addition to its digital products. This provides gamers with a convenient way to access a wide range of products and services, all in one place. The Store's user-friendly interface makes it easy for gamers to browse and purchase products, with various search and filtering options to help users find what they're looking for quickly and easily.
