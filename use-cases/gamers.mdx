## A typical setup

1. **Install the [AI App](https://gamerhash.com/en/app-download)** on your gaming PC. Free, Windows-only.
2. **Run it whenever you want private AI** — chat with Llama, generate images with FLUX, run video clips through LTX. Local, fast, no usage caps.
3. **Toggle on the earning module** before you go to bed or leave for work. Set a GPU usage cap so it doesn't compete with games.
4. **Check the [Mobile app](/apps/mobile)** in the morning — see what your PC earned overnight.
5. **Spend GUSD in the [Store](/apps/store)** — or convert to GHX and HODL.

## Why this beats paid AI services

- Cloud AI (ChatGPT Plus, Midjourney, Sora) costs $20–60/month and caps your usage. Local AI on the same GPU you already own costs nothing extra.
- The same GPU you use for gaming runs **bigger, less censored open-source models** privately.
- You get paid for the hours you're not gaming.

## Edge cases

- **Tournament / competitive gaming setup?** Set a GPU usage cap of 0% during your gaming hours. The earning module won't compete for VRAM.
- **Older GPU?** 8 GB unlocks chat and basic image gen. You won't get the highest-paying earning workloads, but personal-use is solid.
- **Multiple PCs?** Run the AI App on each — earnings consolidate to one account, GHXP accumulates faster.
- **No interest in crypto?** Skip the GHX side entirely. GUSD → store → game keys. Done.
