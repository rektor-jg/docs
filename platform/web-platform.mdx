## Dashboard

Once signed in, the dashboard shows:

- Wallet balances (GHX, GUSD, USDC, legacy BTC) and pending payouts
- Per-device stats from any GamerHash AI App instance attached to your account — uptime, daily/weekly/monthly earnings, historical charts

## Profitability Calculator

Estimate potential earnings at [gamerhash.com/calculator](https://gamerhash.com/en/calculator) before installing the AI App — enter your GPU specs and the calculator returns an expected payout range based on current network conditions.

## GamerHash Store

The [GamerHash Store](https://gamerhash.com/en/shop) is where you spend your earnings. Inventory:

- Games (Steam, Xbox, PlayStation keys)
- In-game currencies
- Gift cards (retail, restaurant, telecom)
- Streaming and entertainment vouchers (Netflix, Spotify, Google Play)

**Payment:** GHX, GUSD, or external wallet GHX (MetaMask). A PIN code from account settings confirms each purchase.

About **85% of users spend their earnings in the Store** rather than withdrawing to external wallets. For partner inquiries about listing inventory, see [Partners — GamerCoin docs](https://docs.gamercoin.com/use-cases/partners).
