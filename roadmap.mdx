## How priorities are set

Roadmap is driven by three signals:

1. **Developer demand** — what models and capabilities API consumers ask for.
2. **AI App contributor feedback** — bug reports and feature requests in the support channels feed directly into the next milestone.
3. **Open-source ecosystem** — when a strong new model lands, integrating it into the Model Center is fast-tracked.
