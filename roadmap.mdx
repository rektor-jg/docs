## Shipped

### 2024

**Q1**
- AI App update — Apple Macbook M2/M3 support (later sunset for non-NVIDIA on AI side)
- AstroGPT upgraded to GPT-4 Turbo (deprecated when local models arrived)
- GHX airdrop for GHXP holders, mobile app update

**Q2**
- **GHXP Boost** launched — higher rewards for GHX held in MetaMask
- New AI Chat dashboard, FAQ in EN
- Earning module first internal tests
- Stats page with device map; GHXP landing page

**Q3**
- Default Chat model switched to **Llama 3.1**; GPU validation fixes
- AI Image Booster prompts (100 GHX paid feature)
- Brand and media kit page
- **Model Center** plus new models: Llama 3.1/3.2, Qwen 2.5, Gemma 2, Stable Diffusion variants, DreamShaper
- USDT payments in the GamerHash Wallet

**Q4**
- New image-gen models: Sana 1.6B, Astro GHX Style LoRA
- LoRA extension packs
- Full redesign of [gamerhash.com](https://gamerhash.com) — desktop and mobile
- **Phi-4** (Microsoft) added; **DeepSeek** text + coder models
- Refreshed homepage and stats nav, translations EN/PL/KO/PT

## How priorities are set

Roadmap is driven by three signals:

1. **deAPI demand** — what models and capabilities developers ask for.
2. **AI App contributor feedback** — the support channels and the in-app surveys feed directly into the next milestone.
3. **Open-source ecosystem** — when a strong new model lands, integrating it into the Model Center is fast-tracked.
