GamerHash turns idle consumer GPUs into productive AI compute. The flagship product, the [**GamerHash AI App**](https://gamerhash.com/en/app-download), lets anyone with an RTX-class GPU run private AI tools (chat, image, video, voice) and earn rewards by sharing spare GPU time with the network. The same network is exposed to developers as [**deAPI**](https://deapi.ai) — a public, OpenAI-compatible API.

## How the GamerHash ecosystem works

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/ecosystem-flow.svg" alt="GamerHash ecosystem flow: contributors run the AI App and share idle GPU power; the DePIN network aggregates it; AI builders and developers consume it via deAPI; revenue flows back as rewards and GHX buy-and-burn." />
</Frame>

Gamers contribute idle GPU time. The DePIN network aggregates it and routes inference, image, and video workloads to free machines. AI builders and developers pay for the resulting compute through the [deAPI](https://deapi.ai) layer or direct integrations. Revenue circulates back as user rewards and GHX buy-and-burn — the loop self-reinforces as more partners join.
