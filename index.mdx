GamerHash transforms idle consumer GPUs into productive AI compute. Our flagship [**GamerHash AI App**](https://gamerhash.com/en/app-download) lets anyone with an RTX-class GPU run private AI tools — chat, image, video, and voice — and earn rewards for sharing idle GPU capacity with the network. Developers can tap into the same network through a public, OpenAI-compatible API.

## How the GamerHash ecosystem works

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/ecosystem-flow.png" alt="GamerHash ecosystem flow: contributors run the AI App and share idle GPU power; the DePIN network aggregates it; AI builders and developers consume it through API integrations and direct AI partnerships; revenue flows back to contributors as rewards — GUSD per workload and GHX distributed through GHXP seasons." />
</Frame>

Gamers contribute idle GPU time. The DePIN network aggregates it and routes inference, image, and video workloads to available machines. AI builders and developers pay for the resulting compute through the API layer or via direct integrations. Revenue flows back to contributors as rewards — and the loop strengthens as more partners join.
