## GamerCoin (GHX)

**GamerCoin (GHX)** is the native token that fuels the **GamerHash ecosystem**. Launched on **Ethereum** , GHX has since expanded with bridges to **BNB Chain** and **Solana** , making it accessible and interoperable across multiple blockchains. Importantly, our whitepaper **was registered** with the **Maltese Financial Services Authority (MFSA)** — highlighting our early commitment to transparency and compliance.

From day one, GHX was designed to reward users and provide real utility. While in the early years it powered mining rewards and purchases in the **GamerHash Store** , today GHX has evolved far beyond its original role.

With the rise of the **GamerHash AI App** , GHX now drives a new economy:

  * rewarding users who share their GPU power for AI computations,

  * enabling access to AI tools within the app,

  * connecting users, AI projects, and Web3 into one ecosystem.


To ensure stability and long-term sustainability, GHX rewards are backed by **AI partners purchasing tokens directly from the market** — aligning incentives and reducing downward price pressure.

The token utility is further expanded with **GHXP (GamerHash Experience Points)** — a complementary mechanism designed to enhance engagement and reward long-term participation in the ecosystem.

### [How to get some precious GHXs?](https://docs.gamercoin.com/gamercoin-ghx-documentation-1/gamercoin-ghx-tokenomics/how-to-get-ghx)

Earning GHX is simple and open to everyone. By running the **GamerHash AI App** , users can share their GPU power and receive GHX as rewards. Tokens can also be earned through community incentives and used directly in the **GamerHash Store**.

For those who prefer traditional trading, GHX is also available on major exchanges — ready to buy, HODL, or use within the ecosystem.

## GamerHash Experience Points (GHXP)

Alongside GHX rewards, participants also collect [**GamerHash Experience Points**](<https://docs.gamercoin.com/gamercoin-ghx-documentation-1/gamerhash-ai/gamerhash-experience-points-ghxp>) for keeping the **GamerHash AI App** running or engaging in in-app activities.

GHXP is **not a token** , but a system of virtual points designed to recognize activity and engagement. Accumulating GHXP strengthens a participant’s standing within the ecosystem and unlocks access to additional rewards, perks, and exclusive opportunities.

This dual-reward model — **GHX for value, GHXP for engagement** — encourages consistent participation and nurtures a strong, active community at the intersection of computing power, blockchain, and AI
