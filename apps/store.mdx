The [GamerHash Store](https://gamerhash.com/en/shop) is the spend side of the platform. It exists so that earnings from the AI App don't have to leave the ecosystem to be useful — most users prefer to convert directly into something they want.

The catalogue is **600+ items** and growing. New offers land monthly, often timed to seasonal sales.

## How to pay

Three currencies accepted at checkout:

- **GUSD** — your in-app stable credit, earned from GPU sharing.
- **GHX** — pay with the token directly. No need to off-ramp first.
- **USDC** — for users who hold stablecoin externally.

Selecting a wallet at checkout requires a PIN (set in account settings). Purchases are instant — digital goods are delivered immediately, no shipping wait.

## Why most users spend in-store

A long-running platform stat: roughly **85% of credited earnings stay in-ecosystem** — users prefer Steam keys and gift cards to off-ramping to fiat. The store exists because it's actually what people want.

For developers and partners, the store is also the route through which GHX gains direct utility — you can pay for real-world goods with the token, not just trade it.
