The **GamerHash Mobile** app is a free companion app for **iOS** and **Android**. It is *not* a mining app and *not* an AI app — your phone is not running workloads. Mobile is the dashboard, store, and wallet for your GamerHash account.

## What it does

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Account management" icon="user-gear">
    Profile settings, KYC, support tickets, password reset.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Live earnings dashboard" icon="chart-line">
    GUSD credited, GHXP balance, payout history per device.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Worker monitoring" icon="server">
    See which of your PCs are online and what they're earning.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Store browsing & purchase" icon="cart-shopping">
    Browse and pay for digital goods directly from your phone.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Wallet transfers" icon="wallet">
    Move GHX or USDC out to external wallets.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Push notifications" icon="bell">
    Workload completion, payouts, store deals, security alerts.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## What it does not do

- **It does not run AI on your phone.** AI workloads need a desktop GPU. Phone hardware can't host them.
- **It does not mine.** The Miner App was wound down in January 2026 anyway, but even when it ran, it never ran on phones.

## Get the app

- **Android** — download from the [Google Play Store](https://gamerhash.com/en/app-download).
- **iOS** — download from the [App Store](https://gamerhash.com/en/app-download).
- Both apps use the same login as your gamerhash.com account.

## Recent updates

- Latest iPhone OS support and stability fixes (Q3 2025).
- USDC payout flow — withdraw earnings out to Binance directly from mobile.
- Multi-device dashboard for users running the AI App on multiple PCs.
