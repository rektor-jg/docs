The **GamerHash Mobile App** is a free companion app for **iOS** and **Android**. Your phone is *not* running AI workloads — Mobile is the dashboard and wallet for your GamerHash account, so you can manage everything without sitting at your PC.

## What's inside

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Account management" icon="user-gear">
    Profile settings and password reset for your GamerHash account.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Live earnings dashboard" icon="chart-line">
    GUSD credited, GHXP balance, payout history per device.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Worker monitoring" icon="server">
    See which of your PCs are online and what they're earning.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Wallet transfers" icon="wallet">
    Move GHX or USDC out to external wallets.
  </Card>
  <Card title="QR-code login" icon="qrcode">
    Scan the QR code shown on the desktop app to sign in fast — same account, no separate credentials.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
